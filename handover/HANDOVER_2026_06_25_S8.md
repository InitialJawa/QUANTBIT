## Architecture notes

### Why this provider order?
- **groq/compound** has the highest quota (no daily cap on Groq free tier) and built-in agent capabilities (uses multiple models internally for compound reasoning). It's the most reliable primary.
- **gemma-4-26b** has 1500 RPD + unlimited TPM on Google AI Studio. The Gemma 4 family is new (June 2026) and very capable. Fresh quota per the user's dashboard.
- **groq llama-3.3-70b** is the backup Groq model. Same provider as primary, but a different model â€” if `groq/compound` 429s, this often still works.
- **openrouter free** is last resort because all free models on OpenRouter are currently rate-limited via shared Venice/Darkbloom pools.

### Why circuit breaker matters
Without it, a 429 from `groq/compound` would consume 1 quota from EVERY provider in the chain (since we try each in order). With 4 providers and frequent 429s, this burns quota fast.

With circuit breaker, a 429 from `groq/compound` puts it in cooldown for 5 min, so the NEXT request skips it entirely. Quota is preserved for the cool-down window.

### Why Gemma 4 over Gemini 2.5 Flash
- `gemini-2.5-flash` has 20 RPD (we hit this limit multiple times)
- `gemma-4-26b` has 1500 RPD, unlimited TPM (much higher)
- Both use same Google AI Studio API
- Gemma 4 is a different model family so has separate quota pools â€” when one hits limit, the other is fresh
