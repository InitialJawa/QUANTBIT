### Bug Fixed During Refactor
**Off-by-one in `extractToolCalls`**: Original regex `\{[\s\S]*?\}` non-greedy consume `}` yang salah di case `{"args": {}}` (empty-object args). Fixed dengan brace-counting algorithm (`findMatchingBrace`) yang scan forward respecting string literals dan nested braces. Test case `parses tool call with nested args` proves the fix.

3. **AI Cockpit pending action queue** — `addPendingAction` already existed in context; now exercised by unit tests (verify no leak across rules).
